Types of Medical Providers Doctors
  • Resident: A doctor who has completed medical school and is in training, called a residency, to be a specialist in care of children (pediatrics).
  • PICU Fellow: A doctor who has completed their training (residency) in pediatrics, and is now in training, called a fellowship, to be a specialist in children with critical illness.
  • PICU Attending: A doctor who has completed residency and fellowship and is now the supervisor of the PICU team.
